Your Journey: The 4-Week Roadmap

Week 1: Data Ingestion & Discovery

Source real-world data from IoT APIs and perform deep Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).

Week 2: Feature Engineering

Convert raw sensor data into meaningful AI inputs. Normalize, scale, and handle imbalances.

Week 3: Modeling & Experimentation

Build ensemble models using PyTorch and Scikit-learn. Validate performance against real benchmarks.

Week 4: Deployment & Storytelling

Turn code into impact. Deploy your model as a FastAPI and present your findings to stakeholders.
